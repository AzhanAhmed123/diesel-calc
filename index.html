Project Overview

This website presents a step-by-step calculator to estimate diesel fuel consumption, CO₂ emissions and savings from solar. It also includes local literature-based recommendations for tree species to mitigate remaining emissions in Karachi.

  • Calculator: 3-step interactive tool.

The Imperative for Hybrid Transition

The Global Carbon Dilemma

As the global community strives to limit atmospheric warming to 1.5°C under the Paris Agreement, decentralized power generation has come under scrutiny. Traditional diesel-only systems release approximately 2.68 kg of CO₂ per liter, creating a "carbon trap" in urban landscapes.

Karachi’s Energy Landscape

Karachi presents a unique case study with 5.5 peak sun hours. Transitioning to a Solar-Diesel Hybrid system allows for a "fuel-displacement" strategy, significantly lowering the payback period while solving a localized energy crisis.

Deep Dive: Understanding the Impact

The Rumble: Diesel's Cost

Diesel generators remain ubiquitous in Karachi's infrastructure. While reliable, they release pollutants like NOx and PM2.5, contributing to severe respiratory illnesses and the urban heat island effect.

The Sun: Karachi's Asset

With abundant irradiance, solar PV offers a silent, sustainable alternative. Displacing diesel consumption with solar power dramatically reduces operational costs and the carbon footprint of NED University and beyond.
